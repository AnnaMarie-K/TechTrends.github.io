Digital Identity

What is Digital Identity?

Digital Identity or Digital ID is a means to secure personal information on the Internet, and can be the main information about a person, company, or divice that is online.

What are the benifits of Digital Identity?

The main benifit to Digital Identity is accessibility; Digital ID makes it easier for people to verify their personal data without having to be in person at the location needing it, which in turn saves time and money.

What are the consequences and/or concernes involving Digital Identity?

The main consequences and/or that involve Digital Identity are the increased risk of Data breches, Identity theft, and major privacy concernes in general.

What components of technology are required for Digital Identity?

Technology Components that are required for Digital Identity are Biometrics to get and use the personal data of the user.

What are some technology careers that are related to Digital Identity?

  • Digital Identity Management
  • Digital Identity Cybersecurity
